Vaccine Eligibility

Everyone in Australia can get vaccinated, as long as they are over the age of 12.
You will get two doses of the same vaccine and are free of charge.

To be fully vaccinated, you must receive 2 doses of the same vaccine. You will get your first dose on your first booking, and must schedule another booking to get your second dose.

You may only get a second dose of another vaccine if there has been an extreme allergic reaction to the supposed vaccine.

The Moderna vaccine, eligibility for the Moderna vaccine applies to those over the age of 12. Bookings for Moderna can be found through here

Priority Groups

If you are within the following priority groups, please ensure that you inform your vaccination provider so that you can receive your vaccination as soon as possible:

  • Aged and disability care workers
  • indiviudals with a disability
  • Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people
  • indiviudals who are pregnant
  • indiviudals with underlying medical conditions
